1. What is our total spending this year?
2. How many invoices have been paid vs pending?
3. What is the average invoice value for paid vs pending invoices?
4. Show me the total spending by category.

## LEVEL 2: Advanced Vendor Analytics
5. Which vendors have the highest average invoice amounts and how many invoices did they send?

7. What is the total spend per vendor for the last quarter?

## LEVEL 3: Complex Business Intelligence

9. What is the month-over-month growth in invoice volume?

## LEVEL 4: Advanced SQL Challenges

11. What is the spending difference between Q3 and Q4 2024 by category?
12. Show me the median invoice amount by vendor category for paid invoices.

14. Which vendor has shown the most consistent invoice frequency over the last 6 months?

## LEVEL 5: Business Intelligence Edge Cases

16. Identify any unusual spending patterns or invoice amounts that might need review.
